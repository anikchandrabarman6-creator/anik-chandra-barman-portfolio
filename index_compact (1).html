About Me

Engineering Growth, Not Guessing.

My journey into Meta Ads started with a frustration. I kept watching ambitious founders burn through advertising budgets — running campaigns without ever knowing why things worked or didn't. Numbers in the dashboard, but no answers in the bank account.

That obsession to understand the why turned me into a Meta Ads specialist. I discovered that real growth doesn't come from hacks or one clever audience. It comes from combining strategy, creative direction, audience science, and uncompromised data integrity into one system.

"Don't scale broken systems. Build the foundation, then scale what works."

I help eCommerce brands not just run ads — I help them understand exactly what's happening in their ad account and why, then systematically turn it into profit.
